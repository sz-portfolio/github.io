About Me

My name is Sebastien Zunec and I'm a computer science student. I was born in Rijeka (Croatia) but I currently live and work in Lausanne (Switzerland). I speak English, fluent French and Croatian. After discovering that my interest in computer science goes beyond mere affinity I have decided to change my profession as a traffic technician. Therefore, after mastering the basics of HTML, CSS and Javascript I started following online courses on the Udemy platform. In following period I decided to learn other programming languages including the basics of PHP. At the beginning of 2022, I took application exam of school „42“ where I learned the basics of programming in the programming language C. Unfortunately, due to some private reasons I had to give up enrolling in school and I am forced apply next year again. In the meantime, I created several websites and webshops which you can check out via links posted on this website. I am currently looking for an internship or a junior position as a frontend developer in order to improve my knowledge in the field of computer science. I hope you will recognize my motivation to work in the mentioned field and my ability to do the same and contact me for any questions you may have.
